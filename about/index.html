About AquaSmart

AquaSmart is a Seychelles-based water solutions business delivering professional installations, testing, and project consultancy.

Who we are

AquaSmart provides water solutions designed to improve quality, comfort, and reliability for homes and projects across Seychelles.

Our approach is simple: assess properly, install professionally, and support you after the job is done.

What we do best
  • Filtration system installation
  • Chlorine removal while keeping essential minerals
  • Private lab water testing and clear recommendations
  • Source detection, connection, and irrigation solutions
