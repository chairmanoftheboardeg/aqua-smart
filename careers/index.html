No uploads. Paste a shareable link to your CV/Resume (Google Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive).

How to get a CV link (quick)
  1. Google Drive: Right click your CV → Share → “Anyone with the link” → Copy link.
  2. Dropbox: Share → Create link → Copy.
  3. OneDrive: Share → Anyone with link can view → Copy link.
